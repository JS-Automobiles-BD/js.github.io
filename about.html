About Us

JS Automobiles Ltd has started its journey to provide world class product services since 2011 alongside with principle of honesty. We started to import various types of filters like oil filter, fuel filter, air filter, AC filters etc. for private motor vehicles. It is most important to replace any spare parts in Japan made cars with Japan made spares which improves overall service quality and longevity. To ensure the best quality of spares, JS Automobiles imports spares (all types of filters) from well renowned manufacturer in Japan, JNB Corporation PTE of Tokyo, Japan. For it's outstanding sales quantity JS Automobiles has achieved "Exclusive Agent" for Bangladesh for the company. In consequence of successful marketing of JS Automobiles in Bangladesh, we also introduce another dependable product of JNB Corporation "NiBK" branded Brake Pad- Brake Shoe and world class horn "Best Horn".
